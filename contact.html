Common Questions

Before You Enquire

+
How do I know a watch from HOROLOGIUM is authentic?
Every piece in our collection passes a 47-point authentication protocol conducted by our master horologists. Each watch is accompanied by a numbered HOROLOGIUM Certificate of Authenticity and full provenance documentation. We have never sold a single inauthentic piece in 120 years — and we never will.
+
Do you buy watches from private collectors?
Yes. We offer both outright purchase and consignment arrangements for fine timepieces. Our acquisition team evaluates every piece on its merits. We pay particular attention to documented provenance, original service records, and accompanying boxes and papers. Please use the enquiry form to begin a confidential conversation.
+
Can I visit without a prior appointment?
All four of our ateliers operate primarily by appointment to ensure that each client receives our full and undivided attention. While we will always do our best to accommodate walk-in visitors during opening hours, we strongly recommend scheduling an appointment to guarantee the time and expertise your enquiry deserves.
+
Do you offer servicing or restoration?
We maintain relationships with certified independent watchmakers and several authorised service centres across Switzerland and the UK. For watches purchased from HOROLOGIUM, we can facilitate servicing introductions and will manage the process on your behalf. We do not operate our own service workshop.
+
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We ship worldwide via fully insured, secure courier — exclusively with specialist luxury watch logistics partners. All shipments are fully insured for their certified value and require a verified signature upon delivery. We never ship via standard postal services.
+
How does the Private Client Programme work?
The Private Client Programme provides a dedicated senior advisor, pre-market access to incoming pieces, auction representation, annual collection valuation, and personal introductions to manufacture visits. It is available by invitation or approved application. We maintain no more than 40 active Private Client relationships globally. To apply, please indicate your interest in the enquiry form above.
+
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank wire transfer (preferred), cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum), and major credit cards for transactions under $25,000. For significant acquisitions, we can facilitate tailored payment arrangements through our private banking partners. All transactions are conducted with absolute confidentiality.
+
Can you source a specific reference I am looking for?
Absolutely — this is one of our most valued services. Our network spans private collections, estate sales, trusted dealers, and manufacturer contacts across 40 countries. We have successfully sourced pieces believed to be entirely unobtainable. If a watch exists and can be acquired legitimately, we will find it. Please describe your requirements in detail using the form above.
